Welcome to Motar Bobar – Your Reliable Partner on the Road!

More Than Just Renting:

At Motar Bobar, we're more than just a car rental company; we're your gateway to a journey of comfort, convenience, and cost-effective travel. Founded with the vision of simplifying transportation for all, we specialize in providing a diverse fleet of top-quality SUVs, Sedans, and Vans, catering to all your travel needs, whether it's a family vacation, a business trip, or a leisure outing.

Tailored Rental Experience:

Understanding the unique needs of each customer, we offer personalized rental solutions. Whether you need a car for a day, a week, or longer, our flexible rental plans are designed to accommodate your schedule seamlessly.

Membership Program – Travel More, Save More:

Join our exclusive membership program and unlock a world of benefits. Members enjoy discounted rates, priority service, and exclusive offers. Our membership program is our way of thanking you for your loyalty and trust in us.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a wide range of vehicles to suit various needs, including sedans for city travel, SUVs for rugged terrain, and vans for group trips or larger cargo space.

Booking is simple! Visit our website, choose your vehicle type, select your rental dates, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your reservation.

Renters must be at least 21 years old, possess a valid driver's license, and have a credit card in their name. Additional documentation may be required for international renters.

Standard rentals come with a daily mileage limit, but unlimited mileage options are available for an additional fee. Please check the specific terms for your rental.

Yes, you can book a car for someone else, but the person driving must meet our rental requirements and be present to sign the rental agreement at the time of vehicle pickup.

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time without any charges. Cancellations made later may incur a fee.

Yes, we offer various insurance options for added peace of mind. These include collision damage waiver, personal accident insurance, and more.

Additional drivers are welcome but must be registered at the time of rental and meet all rental requirements.

In the event of an accident or breakdown, contact our 24/7 roadside assistance immediately. Emergency contact details are provided with your rental documents.

Pets are allowed in our vehicles, but we request that they be kept in carriers for the duration of the trip to maintain vehicle cleanliness.
